Indian Classical Dance School

SamarpanNrithyalaya a leading school to teach and promote Indian art and culture in South Wales.

We teach Bharathanatyam and focus on students to develop skills to become lifelong learners in the art form. We prepare students for various performances and examinations conducted by the OEBL board. We are also approved for Art Awards, UK.


Dr Leena Menon

Founder and Director


Leena is a Doctor by profession with a love for dance since an early age. She completed her arangetram under the tutelage of Late Guru Smt Padmini Ramachandranin 1986 and has several performances to her credit.
